Arthik AI

Your earnings. One dashboard. Zero confusion.

🚨 Problem

India’s creator economy is booming, yet millions of content creators and small businesses—especially in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities—struggle to manage multiple platforms and fragmented income streams. This leads to lost revenue, delayed payments, and uncertainty in scaling digital careers.

💡 Solution

Arthik AI is an AI-powered dashboard that unifies all social media accounts and revenue streams in one place. It tracks analytics, consolidates monetization, and provides AI-driven insights to help creators save time, reduce financial uncertainty, and grow sustainably.

🤖 Use of OpenAI APIs

  • GPT: Summarizes comments, analyzes sentiment, and generates growth tips.
  • Whisper: Transcribes video/audio for engagement insights.
  • DALL·E: Suggests thumbnails or visuals for higher engagement.

🔄 How It Works

  1. Connect social media accounts (YouTube, Instagram, etc.)
  2. Data collected: views, likes, comments, revenue stats
  3. AI analysis: insights, sentiment, growth suggestions
  4. Dashboard output: earnings split, savings %, tax advice

⚙️ Tech Stack

  • Frontend: ReactJS / Streamlit
  • Backend: Node.js / Python
  • Deployment: Heroku
  • AI: OpenAI GPT, Whisper, DALL·E

🌍 Impact & Vision

Arthik AI empowers Indian creators to turn their passion into reliable income. By simplifying monetization and analytics, we help them save hours each week, reduce uncertainty, and unlock new growth opportunities—especially in Tier 2/3 towns.
